Connaissance

Science is all about asking questions and finding answers…
Have you ever asked yourself how good you are at science? For the first time in the history of Odyssey, we present to you, a science event, “Connaissance” where you can find the answer. Make sure you’re the smartest, the wittiest and the sharpest, as the search won’t be easy!

Specifications:
Venue: second floor, room opposite to the science lab
Timings:
Each school can send one team of two people only.

Rules:
There will be three rounds. No prior preparation is required for any of them.
Round one- ink the theory. Teams will choose two theories which they need to link together and create a thesis. Only five teams will qualify to round two.
Use of any reference books is prohibited.
Round two and three will be quizzes.

Judging criteria:
Scientific correctness
Creativity
