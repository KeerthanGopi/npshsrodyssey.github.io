Invivtus

Acquire some of the most popular cryptocurrencies on the internet in a Monopoly-styled quiz, charge players network fees for landing on your cryptos and collect money. Use your money to acquire more cryptocurrencies, build mining rigs and farms and become a CRYPTO GIANT.

Each pair starts off with a purse of $10,000 each.
The participants play the game with a symbol representing them. They play the game on a projected screen.
The number of places to be moved is decided by the team. They answer questions based on the number of steps they need to move (the higher the steps to be moved, the harder the questions will be).
After the first round, the top 2 pairs are split into 4 individual participants for round 2 who would play the same game but with a purse of $500 and harder crises.
