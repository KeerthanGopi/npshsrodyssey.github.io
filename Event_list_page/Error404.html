Error 404

ABOUT THE EVENT:
“Error-404” is a computer-based event built upon the principle of finding efficient solutions to everyday problems. Each question provides a new topic and requires deep analysis and understanding of the subject.
The competition requires the participants to solve questions with varying range of difficulty in the given time. They must provide efficient codes for the problems given and the solutions will be judged according to accuracy. Either full points will be awarded for a question or zero points. There will be no partial marking. The programs provided at the end of the competition will be considered as final. The team which scores the maximum will be declared as the winner. In case of a tie, the team which has solved the question with the most points wins.

RULES AND REGULATIONS:
• Each school is permitted to send one team with three members each.
• Use of personal computers, mobile phones, pen drives and other external storage devices are not allowed.
• The teams will use the computers provided by our school only.
• Use of internet during the event is not allowed.
• The students can program in three languages:
     o C++
     o Java
     o Python
• The event will be for 3.5 hours, which includes lunch break.
Further information about the event will be informed to the participants on the day of the fest.
