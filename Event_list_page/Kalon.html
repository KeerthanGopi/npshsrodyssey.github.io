Kalon

“Beauty begins the moment you decide to be yourself.”
Do you have what it takes to be Ms. /Mr. Odyssey? Are you talented, competent, lucid, eloquent, chic, à la mode and in vogue - all at once? Does your charisma have the power to influence change? Do your thoughts speak to people?
If yes then Kalon is the event for you. Kalon is a personality pageant that has three rounds. “Minute to Win It” (Talent Round), Extempore Speaking and Ramp Walk. Participate and show the world that you have the potential to become the next Mr./Ms. Odyssey.

Specifications:
Venue:
Round 1 and 2: Rooms (To be allocated)(12B and 12C)
Round 3: Central Courtyard
Timings: 10: 30 onwards
Number of participants – 2 per school (1 boy and 1 girl)
Individual Event

Rules:

Round 1 – “Minute to Win It”:
Talent round: 1 minute per participant.
Judging Criteria: talent, maintaining time limit.

Round 2 - Extempore:
2 minutes to prepare.
2 minutes speech per participant.
Judging criteria: maintaining time limit, content, delivery.

Round 3 - Ramp Walk:
Theme: Express Yourself
Finalists will walk the ramp, introduce themselves to the judges and justify their claim to the title.
There will also be a Question and Answer Session with the judges.
Participants have to do their own hair and make-up for the last round.
Judging criteria: Fashion sense, confidence, communication skills.
Note: Any sort of obscenity or vulgarity in the clothes will lead to immediate disqualification.
