Cucinando

“The recipe has no soul, you as a cook must bring soul to the recipe”. Bring out the best of your culinary skills through our event at NPS HSR, Cucinando. Two interesting rounds to bring out your proficiency in cooking and contest with other schools.

SPECIFICATIONS:
Venue : Math Lab, 3rd Floor
Timings : 10:30am to 01:30pm
No. Of participants per team : 3

DETAILS:
ROUND 1 : Vegetable carving
ROUND 2: Preparation of main dish

Participants need to bring a write -up regarding the ingredients used and nutritive value of the main dish.

NOTE: A secret ingredient will be given at the beginning of Round 2. The same needs to be incorporated as one of the ingredients in the main dish. Teams will be judged on the effective incorporation of the secret ingredient.

JUDGING CRITERIA:

ROUND 1:
Presentation
Creativity
Accuracy

ROUND 2:
Creativity
Presentation
Nutritive value
Incorporation of secret ingredient
